Our Mission

At Self-Health, our mission is to contribute to the fight against the COVID-19 pandemic by leveraging cutting-edge technology and data-driven insights. We are committed to developing accurate and reliable models for the early detection of COVID-19 cases, empowering healthcare professionals and communities to make informed decisions.

Who We Are

Self-Health website is a dedicated team of AMC ISE and technology enthusiasts united by a common goal: using predictive modeling to enhance our understanding and response to the challenges posed by COVID-19. Established in 2024, we have been at the forefront of harnessing the power of machine learning to address the evolving dynamics of the pandemic.

What Sets Us Apart

Our approach to COVID-19 prediction is rooted in a combination of state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms, a robust feature set that goes beyond traditional parameters, and a continuous refinement process that integrates the latest research and data. We are driven by a passion for innovation and a shared determination to contribute meaningfully to the global effort against COVID-19.
